About Us

LeisurePace


"LeisurePace" conveys a leisurely and relaxed running experience. "Leisure" represents leisure or relaxation, while "Pace" refers to rhythm or speed. Overall, this name conveys the core concept of super slow jogging—enjoying exercise at a leisurely pace while focusing on health and pleasure.
This name also suggests slowing down and allowing people to step away from the busy pace of life, experiencing the joy of running at a more peaceful stride. Choosing this name helps to establish a positive, pleasant image in the minds of users, making them willing to choose this app to achieve their relaxed jogging goals.
